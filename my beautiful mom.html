About my mom

Let me tell you a story about my mother.My mother's name is Che wangi Binti Bakar, but everyone calls her "Mah.".

She is Malay and speaks both English and Malay.My mother is short and slim, with brown eyes

My mother is 51 years old and her favourite colours are green and yellow.

My mother really likes to collect various types of caladiums and flowers.

She's the last child of three siblings.

She is a very kind person so by her, I have learnt to be more kind to people.
